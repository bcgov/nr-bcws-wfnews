Excluded Areas

Restrictions do not include areas which are within the boundaries of local government and subject to local bylaws. You should check with local government authorities before lighting any fires.

Warning

The Wildfires Act and the Wildfire Regulation grant authority to government officials engaged in fire control (including firefighters) to order all people to leave active fire areas.

A person must not remain in or enter the restricted area without the prior written authorization of an official designated for the purposes of the Wildfire Act.
