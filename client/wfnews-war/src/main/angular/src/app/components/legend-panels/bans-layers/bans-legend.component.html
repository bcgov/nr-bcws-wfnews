Fire Bans
Category 1 (Campfires)

A controlled burn of 0.5 metres high by 0.5 metres wide or smaller, conducted for recreational, ceremonial, or cooking purposes.

 Category 2 Open Fires

A controlled burn of 1 or 2 fires, each no larger than 2 meters high by 3 meters wide, or stubble and grass burning over an area that does not exceed 0.2 hectares.

 Category 3 Open Fires

A controlled burn that meets any of the following criteria: is larger than 2 metres high by 3 metres wide, 3 or more piles are being burned concurrently, 1 or more burning windrows, or stubble and grass burning over an area greater than 0.2 hectares.
