Bans and Prohibitions

Category 3 Open Fires

  • Any fire Larger than 2 metres high by 3 metres wide
  • 3 or more concurrently burning piles each no larger than 2 metres height and 3 metres wide
  • 1 or more burning rows windrose
  • Stuble or grass burning over an area greater than 0.2 hectares

Category 2 Open Fires

  • 1 to 2 concurrently burning piles each no larger than 2 metres height and 3 metres wide
  • Stuble or grass burning over an area greater than 0.2 hectares

Campfires

  • Any fire smaller than 0.5 metres high by 0.5 metres wide
